# Supply and Issuance

QZN has a fixed and immutable maximum supply.

- Max Supply: 676,000,000 QZN
- Minting: One-time genesis mint
- Inflation: None
- Reissuance: None
- Price: 250 QUBIC

All QZN entering circulation originates from genesis allocations and recycled ecosystem activity. No additional tokens may be created under any circumstances.

Supply reduction occurs only through permanent burn mechanisms tied to arcade usage.
