# Locking and Progression

QZN may be locked to unlock access, permissions, and status within the arcade ecosystem.

Locking QZN does not provide:
- Yield
- Profit share
- Financial returns

Lock tiers unlock features such as:
- Early access to games
- Tournament hosting permissions
- Creator tooling
- Prestige and recognition

All locked QZN remains non-yielding and subject to defined unlock periods.
