# Genesis Distribution

The genesis supply of QZN is allocated to support long-term arcade ecosystem operations.

- 30% — Arcade Treasury (events, seasons, rewards)
- 20% — Builder and Creator Pool
- 15% — Community and Tournament Programs
- 15% — Liquidity and Market Health
- 10% — Ecosystem Grants
- 10% — Team and Core Contributors (vested)

All allocations are intended to fund participation, creation, and infrastructure rather than speculation.
