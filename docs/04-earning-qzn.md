# Earning QZN

QZN is earned exclusively through verifiable contribution to the arcade ecosystem.

Eligible activities include:
- Skill-based gameplay and achievements
- Tournament participation and placement
- Game, level, or asset creation
- Community moderation and hosting
- Seasonal and time-limited events

Anti-abuse mechanisms include:
- Daily earning caps
- Skill-weighted reward distribution
- Vesting delays prior to unlock
- Progressive unlock rates based on participation tier
